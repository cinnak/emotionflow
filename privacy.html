Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Our Commitment

EmotionFlow is designed with privacy at its core. Your emotional data is deeply personal, and we believe it should stay that way.

Data We Don't Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process:

  • The text you type in the emotion input field
  • Your emotional state or emotion selections
  • Personal information of any kind
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies for advertising or profiling

Local Storage

EmotionFlow uses your browser's localStorage to save:

  • Release history — timestamps, emotion type, and text previews (stored only on your device)
  • Release count — a simple counter of how many times you've used the app
  • Preferences — sound on/off, reduced motion settings

This data never leaves your browser. It is not transmitted to any server. You can clear it at any time using the "Clear History" button or by clearing your browser data.

Third-Party Services

EmotionFlow loads the Inter font from Google Fonts. Google may collect anonymized usage data through this service. No other third-party services are used.

No Accounts

EmotionFlow does not require accounts, logins, or registration of any kind.

Children's Privacy

EmotionFlow is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as features evolve. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at privacy@emotionflow.app
